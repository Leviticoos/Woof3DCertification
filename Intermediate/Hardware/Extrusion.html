Extrusion

Understanding when a print is being over-extruded is an important skill. It requires a good understanding of the printer’s settings and the filament used. Over-extruding is when the printer extrudes too much material. Some issues over extruding can cause stringing, blobs on the print, jams, and dimensional inaccuracy. Avoiding over-extrusion is the best solution, and some of those are decreasing print temperature and checking filament diameter and flow rate settings. Checking your filament settings, print temperature, and flow rate settings can help ensure over-extrusion won’t stop you in your tracks from making high-quality prints.
