What is an Intermediant?

The intermediate 3D printer should have more experience with unexpected events and a bit of both theory and practical knowledge that can help them diagnose problems. 3D printing is an art more than a science, and where a beginner can follow directions and use a well-oiled machine, and an intermediate can start to help keep printers running.
