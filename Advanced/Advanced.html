What defines 'Advanced' 3D printing?

Advanced 3D printers can run the show. Upgrades, difficult troubleshooting, and custom solutions are part of this level of knowledge. While they might not be able to build a printer from scratch, they understand the parts of a printer, what they do, how they can go wrong, and how to fix them.
